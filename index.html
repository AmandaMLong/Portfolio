Greater Sage-grouse as an Umbrella Species

×

As first author on a publication (in prep), A Pythonic tool to locate high-priority conservation areas through mining BBS: Sage-grouse as an umbrella species, I used Python to obtain data via the Breeding Bird Survey (BBS) and investigate the influence Greater Sage-grouse (GSG) management has on metrics of bird diversity from 1995-2019 (i.e., bird diversity, species richness, rarity weighted richness, and Jaccard index).

This research helped land managers and conservationists better plan to protect GSG as an umbrella species to protect biodiversity.

Skills

Metrics of Bird Diversity, Umbrella Species Concept

Software

ArcGIS Pro, Excel, Python, R

Data

Breeding Bird Survey

Positive Responses of Tiger Populations to the Global Tiger Recovery Program

×

As first author on a publication (in prep), Tiger (Panthera tigris) Endangerment: Positive Responses to Tiger Conservation Efforts, I reviewed pertinent literature to collect fecundity and survivorship data for 3 distinct tiger subpsecies (Liu et al. 2018): Amur (P.t. altacia), Bengal (P.t. tigris), and Sunda (P.t. sumatrae).

As a flagship and umbrella species, assessing the PVA of tigers aids in the assessment of global conservation efforts and understanding the overall health, integrity, and sustainability of larger ecosystems (Global Tiger Recovery Program 2010).

Skills

Population Viability Analysis,

Software

ArcGIS Pro, Excel, R

Data

IUCN, Tiger Fecundity and Survivorship
