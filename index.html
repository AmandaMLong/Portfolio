Delineating Patch-Corridor-Matrix Boundaries

×

As part of my MS thesis research, Conservation, Connectivity, and Coexistence: Understanding Corridor Efficacy in Fragmented Landscapes, I delineated landscape elements along contiguous forest and rangeland edges in Kansas, Idaho, and Czechia integrating field data with remotely sensed data.

Skills

Digitization (heads-up), Remote Sensing, Vegetation Analysis

Software

ArcGIS Pro, ArcMap, Excel, GoogleEarth

Data

GPS coordinates, Landsat 8 OLI/TIRS

Identifying Corridor Termini

×

Centerline of de facto corridor complex in Kansas, US. where the greatest percent change in width was used to identify transitional areas of patch-corridor interfaces between two widest and narrowest points. Point 1 and 2 represent where patch one transitions into the corridor while point 3 and 4 represent where the corridor transitions into patch two. The corridor was delineated from the inner points (2, 3) tapered to the outer points (1, 4).
